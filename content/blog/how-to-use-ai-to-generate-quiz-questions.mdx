## What is an AI Quiz Generator

An AI quiz generator is a tool that automatically creates questions based on a topic, chapter, or text you provide.

Instead of writing questions manually, you simply enter a prompt like:

> “Create 10 MCQs from photosynthesis for grade 9 students”

The AI then generates a complete quiz including:
- Multiple choice questions  
- True/false questions  
- Short conceptual questions  
- Answers and explanations

## How AI Quiz Generators Work

AI quiz tools follow a simple process:

1. You enter a topic or text  
2. AI analyzes the content  
3. It identifies key concepts  
4. It generates questions and answers  
5. It formats them into a quiz structure  

This entire process happens in seconds.

## How to Create AI Quiz Questions (Step-by-Step)

Select a clear topic like:
